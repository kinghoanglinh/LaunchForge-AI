## Description

```text
DealScope Agent is a complete startup due diligence workflow for founders, angel investors, accelerator mentors, and Web3 builders. It turns startup ideas, pitches, or company descriptions into structured analyst-style diligence packages with market analysis, customer pain, competitive moat, team assessment, business model review, go-to-market analysis, risk register, red-team critique, fundraising readiness, validation experiments, investor questions, assumptions log, and a calibrated GO / CONDITIONAL GO / NO-GO verdict.

Unlike a plain prompt, DealScope Agent includes helper tools for startup profile normalization, input completeness scoring, SEA/Vietnam context detection, diligence question generation, memo quality validation, and structured output packaging. It does not provide financial, legal, tax, or investment advice and does not invent unsupported market facts.
```

## Use Cases

### Create complete startup diligence memos

Turn a raw startup idea, pitch, or company description into a structured analyst-style memo with market, moat, team, business model, risk, and verdict sections.

### Red-team a startup thesis

Stress-test the strongest argument against a startup, likely failure modes, hidden assumptions, and evidence that would change the view.

### Assess fundraising readiness

Evaluate whether a startup is not ready, pre-seed narrative ready, seed-ready if proof points hold, or a strong fundraising candidate.

### Build validation experiment plans

Convert uncertainty into experiments with hypotheses, methods, success metrics, timelines, and failure signals.
