## Description

```text
DealScope Pro is a deeper startup due diligence prompt for founders, angel investors, accelerator mentors, and startup operators. It expands DealScope AI with a richer investor-style memo, red-team critique, fundraising readiness score, go-to-market review, validation experiment plan, investor questions, assumptions log, and a GO / CONDITIONAL GO / NO-GO verdict.

It is designed for serious first-pass startup evaluation and founder preparation. DealScope Pro does not pull live data and does not provide financial, legal, tax, or investment advice. It helps users structure their thinking, identify missing evidence, and prepare better diligence questions.
```

## Use Cases

### Prepare for investor conversations

Founders can turn a startup pitch into investor objections, fundraising readiness analysis, and the proof points needed before outreach.

### Red-team a startup thesis

Operators and investors can stress-test the strongest argument against a startup, likely failure modes, and hidden assumptions.

### Build a validation plan

Teams can convert a startup idea into fast validation experiments with hypotheses, methods, success metrics, and failure signals.

### Create a deeper diligence memo

Angels, scouts, and accelerator mentors can generate a more detailed first-pass memo than the free DealScope AI version.
