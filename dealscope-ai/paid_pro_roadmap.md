# DealScope Pro Roadmap

DealScope v1 should launch free. The paid version should wait until the Swarms
account is eligible for paid listings and the free prompt has enough feedback.

## Eligibility Gate

Launch paid only after:

- at least 2 published Swarms items
- average rating is 3.5+
- DealScope free version has real usage or review feedback
- v1 output quality issues are fixed

## DealScope Pro Positioning

Name:

```text
DealScope Pro
```

Positioning:

```text
Deeper startup due diligence prompt with richer scoring rubrics, investor memo
format, red-team critique, fundraising readiness analysis, and follow-up
question generation.
```

## Pro Features

- deeper market timing analysis
- red-team section that attacks the thesis
- fundraising readiness score
- investor question generator
- pitch deck gap analysis
- customer discovery interview plan
- competitor map template
- validation experiment plan
- more detailed SEA/Vietnam market lens
- output variants for founder, angel, accelerator, and Web3 builder personas

## Upgrade Path

| Phase | Product | Price | Purpose |
|---|---|---:|---|
| v1 | DealScope AI Free | Free | Reviews, trust, discovery |
| v1.1 | DealScope AI Free update | Free | Improve based on feedback |
| Pro | DealScope Pro Prompt | $5 | Paid prompt with deeper workflow |
| Agent | DealScope Agent | $15-$25 | Multi-step workflow and richer output |

## Paid Listing Description Draft

```text
DealScope Pro is a deeper startup due diligence prompt for founders, angel
investors, and accelerator mentors. It expands the free DealScope workflow with
red-team critique, fundraising readiness scoring, pitch deck gap analysis,
customer discovery questions, validation experiment planning, and richer
investor-style memo structure.
```

## Do Not Include In Paid Claims

Avoid:

- guaranteed returns
- "VC-grade" as a guarantee
- "invest / do not invest" instructions
- live-data claims unless a future agent actually browses or calls APIs
- legal, tax, financial, or investment advice claims
