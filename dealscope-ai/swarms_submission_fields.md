## Description

```text
DealScope AI is a structured startup due diligence prompt for founders, angel investors, accelerator mentors, and Web3 builders. Provide a startup name, pitch, or brief company description, and DealScope generates an analyst-style memo covering market intelligence, competitive moat, team and execution, business model, unit economics, risk flags, assumptions, and a GO / CONDITIONAL GO / NO-GO verdict.

It is designed for fast first-pass evaluation, founder self-review, investor pre-screening, accelerator feedback, and SEA/Vietnam-aware startup analysis. DealScope does not pull live data and does not provide financial, legal, tax, or investment advice. It flags missing information, separates assumptions from facts, and helps users decide what to investigate next.
```

## Use Cases

### Validate a startup idea before building

Founders can paste a rough startup idea and receive a structured memo that highlights market strength, risks, missing assumptions, and whether the idea deserves further validation.

### Pre-screen inbound startup pitches

Angel investors and scouts can turn a short pitch into a first-pass due diligence memo with market, moat, team, business model, and risk analysis.

### Create accelerator mentor feedback

Mentors can use DealScope to produce a consistent feedback framework for portfolio startups, including next due diligence questions and validation steps.

### Analyze SEA/Vietnam startup opportunities

Builders operating in Southeast Asia can get analysis that considers local market dynamics when relevant, while clearly separating assumptions from verified facts.
