# Review Seeding Plan

Goal: earn honest early feedback and marketplace reviews without violating trust
or promising rewards for positive ratings.

## Target Review Group

Start with 10 testers:

- 3 founders
- 2 angel investors or scouts
- 2 accelerator mentors or startup operators
- 2 Web3 builders
- 1 SEA/Vietnam startup operator

## Outreach Message

```text
Hey, I just launched DealScope AI, a free startup due diligence prompt on Swarms.

It turns a startup idea or pitch into a structured analyst-style memo with
market, moat, team, business model, risks, assumptions, and a GO / CONDITIONAL
GO / NO-GO verdict.

Could you test it with one startup idea and leave honest feedback? Negative or
critical feedback is useful too. I am using the first reviews to improve v1.1.

Link: [Swarms listing link]
GitHub: https://github.com/kinghoanglinh/LaunchForge-AI/tree/prompt/dealscope-ai
```

## Feedback Questions

Ask testers:

1. Was the memo useful within 2 minutes?
2. Did the verdict feel too harsh, too soft, or about right?
3. Did the assumptions log make missing information clear?
4. Did the risk register identify real risks?
5. What would make this worth paying for?

## First 14 Days

| Day | Action | Goal |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | Publish free prompt | Live listing |
| 1-2 | Send to 10 testers | First usage |
| 3-5 | Collect output screenshots and feedback | Identify prompt issues |
| 6-7 | Update prompt v1.1 if needed | Higher quality |
| 8-14 | Share examples on X/Discord | Organic usage |

## Review Ethics

Do:

- ask for honest feedback
- invite criticism
- disclose that reviews help improve the product
- share examples only with permission

Do not:

- pay for positive reviews
- ask people to rate 5 stars regardless of experience
- imply that DealScope gives investment advice
- share private startup details without permission
